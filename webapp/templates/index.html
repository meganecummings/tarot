Welcome to Your Personal Tarot Reading

Are you on the precipice of making a decision about your future? Curious about what the fates have in store? It might be time to consult the Tarot for a personal reading.

Before you begin the process, think deeply about a question you may want answered by the cards. After you focus on the question at hand, use your fingers to select the reading of your choice:

Draw 1 Cards:
This option draws One Card, providing a single answer to your heart's inquiry, typically circulating around The Present

Draw 3 Random Cards:
This option provides 3 different cards in response to your question, selecting a response to the past, present and future.

Curious about the Cards?
This Option lists all the cards in the Tarot

This is a simple web application written in Python/Flask to draw one or more randomly selected cards from a dictionary (hash) of 72 Tarot card description and image references.

The source code to this app is here - please feel free to fork or re-use, there are no license restrictions.
